Three challenges I've faced in the past;

✶ One thing I've faced as a challenge in life would have to be the separation between my parents. When I was in the 6th grade, something terrible happened between them and they could no longer be together. This didn't have a positive effect on me or my brother and it put me in depression. I've since been going to therapy and with time, it doesn't really hurt as much but I still think about it and it makes me upset a bit.

❃ Another challenge, as cliché as this sounds, high school was actually a challenge I'm very glad I got through. I didn't really have a good high school experience so moving and onto college and starting anew is much better. Here I get to meet new people and even get better grades than what I had in high school.

☼ The last challenging moment in life is facing depression and anxiety. In life, I go through moments where I feel no energy, where I feel like I'm absolutely worthless and matter to no one, where I believe that it's better if I weren't alive and I feel no motivation to do anything I'm supposed to do. I feel empty and I tend to put myself down and hurt myself with things I think to myself (never physically though). Usually, to get myself out of this I try to cheer myself up and doing something that would usually make me happy or talk to someone about it.
